    Greetings! My name is Jeff Zhang. I'm currently a sophomore at the American University in Washington, DC, studying computer science. I was born in Vancouver, Canada on March 3, 2000, where I lived there till I was seven. Since then, my family resides in the town of Lexington, near Boston, MA.     Ever since I was a child, I've loved drawing; from my childhood heros like Batman to my own characters, I drew to express my imagination. Nowadays, I mostly just draw for leisure and when I have the time, but when I do, I can spend hours upon hours just trying to perfect the little details on my work.     Like drawing, I started doing photography at a young age, but it wasn't until I was 15 I started taking it seriously. Using my dad's Nikon DSLR camera, I took nearly every photograph during family vacations, and when time permitted, doing portrait and scenenic photo shoots with my friends or just on my own. In the future, I will buy my own camera so that I can still do photography when I'm not back home with access to my dad's camera :)     Feel free to explore!
